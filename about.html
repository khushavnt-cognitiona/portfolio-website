About Me

Khushvant Jadhao is a passionate and dedicated Software Engineer with hands-on experience in both backend and full-stack development. He began his professional journey as a Backend Developer Intern at Cognition Technology, Pune from December 2022 to August 2023, where he gained strong expertise in Java, Spring Boot, MySQL, Hibernate, and related backend technologies.

Currently, he is working as a Full-Stack Engineer Intern at ARC Technology and Institute, Nagpur, further enhancing his skills in modern web development and full-stack application design.

Khushvant holds a Master of Computer Applications (MCA) degree specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning from Chandigarh University. Prior to this, he completed his B.Voc in Software Development in 2022 from Amravati University, Amravati, graduating with a commendable CGPA of 8.5.

He is deeply committed to building efficient, scalable, and user-friendly software solutions. With a strong foundation in both backend and full-stack technologies, Khushvant continues to expand his expertise, focusing on creating impactful solutions in AI, ML, and modern web development.
